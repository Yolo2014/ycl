Descriptions
Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitter is an optical distribution device based on the waveguide technology. It is made of a silica plate with compact construction, can be operated on a wide range of wavelength with reliable performance and uniformity, particularly suitable for the connection from central office to the optical network termination in passive optical network, i.e. BPON, EPON, GPON, etc.
Applications
FTTx
Analog / Digital Passive Optical Network
Local Area Network / Wide Area Network / Metropolition Area Network
Cable TV Network
