B.1000M Types Optical Fiber Media Converter

Features

  • Stable switch power supply, guarantee the products long time working, convenience for changing power.
  • Support IEEE802.1Q and ISL and backbone connection
  • 1000Mbps operation speed
  • Support crossover cord and direct cord, self-adapt function

Technical parameter

Protocols

IEEE802.3Z

Interface

RJ-45(1000Base-TX) SC(1000Base-FX)

Indicator

POW:

It's on when power supply normally;
It's off when power off or power supply wrong.

FX/ACT:

Fiber Transmission Indicator. It's on when connection line is correct; It's flashing when transmitting data. It's off when connection wrong.

TP NK:

Raster Line Indicator. It's on when connection line is correct; It's flashing when transmitting data. It's off when connection wrong.

DUP:

It's on when working under full- duplex;
It's off when working under half-duplex.

1000M:

It's on when working under 1000M; It's off when working under other speed.

100M:

It's on when working under 100M; It's off when working under other speed.

10M:

It's on when working under 10M; It's off when working under other speed.
