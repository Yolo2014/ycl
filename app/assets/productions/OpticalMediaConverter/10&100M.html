A.10/100M Types Optical Fiber Media Converter

Features

  • Stable switch power supply, guarantee the products long time working, convenience for changing power.
  • 10/100Mbps self-adapt, full-duplex or half duplex
  • Support crossover cord and direct cord, self-adapt function

Technical parameter

Protocols

IEEE802.3U IEEE802.3

Interface

RJ-45(10/100Base-TX)
SC ST or FC(100Base-FX)

Indicator

POW:

Power Indicator

FX100:

Fiber Indicator. It's on when working under 100M; It's off when working wrong.

FX/LINK/ACT:

Fiber Transmission Indicator.
It's on when connection line is correct; It's flashing when transmitting data.
It's off when connection wrong.

TX100:

Raster Line Indicator. It's on when working under 100M; It's off when stop working or working under 10M mode.

TX/LINK/ACT:

Raster Transmission Indicator.
It's on when connection line is correct; 
It's flashing when transmitting data; It's off when connection wrong.

FDX:

It's on when working under full- duplex;
It's off when working under half-duplex.
