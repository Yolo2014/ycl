The official entertainment trip, 2014

Every year YCL organizes entertainment trip for its employee’s recreation. This year YCL organized two days (15th and 16th December) trip to Shaoguan which is located on the Jingguang Railway about 221 KM north of the provincial capital of Guangzhou. Shaoguan is especially famous for Nanhua temple and Danxia mountain. First day, all employees of YCL visited Nanhua Temple which is called “the temple of South China”. This is a historical temple which was first built in A.D.502, more than 1,500 years ago. At evening all employees went to Danxia mountain for hot spring bath in natural hot mountain water. It was an amazing and wonderful experience to enjoy the indoor and outdoor hot spring with “hot ice flame”. End of the first day all get together in a networking dinner party to cheers each other.

Second day at the morning the first trip to the Danxia Mountain, a geological park of the world, lies within the boundaries of Shaoguan, covering an area of 290 square kilometers. It is the most beautiful beauty spot with the largest area since1988. There is Jinjiang river surrounding the mountains and all employees enjoyed the boat journey around places to see the red gravel rocks on the mountain, which look like rosy clouds. After networking lunch all employees climbed the mountain around 900m way. At the evening the trip was being end on the way to home.

The Shaoguan trip let employees share the feelings each other, enhance company staff’s consultative power and at the same time, this trip made us determined and confident to contribute our wisdom for company sake.
